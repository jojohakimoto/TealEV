Economy & Pricing

At Teal we are proud to be transparent. We are not here to over-sell, we are here to work with you on tailoring a product that suits your needs the best.

The calculator below gives a fairly accurate estimation of what a walk-in van or a cab and chassis e-truck will cost. The default values are based on statistical averages from the last mile delivery industry and might not represent your operation/region the best. Adjust the vehicle range and annual mileage and observe the battery size, purchase cost, and payback period to find the sweet spot for your operation.

For best results, input the right fuel prices. Also note electricity rates are highly dependent on the type of service (commercial/residential) and your location.
