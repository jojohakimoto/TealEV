Battery Management System

Battery Management System


Teal’s BMS is developed in house using the automotive grade BMS chips from STMicroelectronics capable of offering a modular or central architecture. Highly precise voltage readings over a wide temperature range allows for a multi-chemistry Li-Ion solution to suit the needs of the most stringent applications. It is customizable to suit the requirements of different applications and seamlessly integrates with all systems designed by Teal.


Highlights


  • AEC-Q100 qualified
  • Full ISO26262 compliant, ASIL-D systems ready
  • Up to 14 cell measurements per module
  • Maximum of ±2mV error in 1.2V to 4.7V voltage range over -40°C to +105°C temperature range
  • 200mA balancing current per cell
  • Robust hot plug performance

Vehicle Control Module

Vehicle Control Module Functionality Overview


Teal’s Vehicle Control Module (VCM) developed in house, provides a fully customizable solution for control over electric vehicle (EV) systems, including drivetrain, charging, and accessory components.


Communications

  • CAN enabled for control of CAN compatible EV system components
  • LIN bus compatible for communicating with LIN accessory systems
  • Capable of handling 5V analog inputs for integration with a wide range of automotive sensors
  • Compatible with wireless keyless entry systems

Main Functionalities

  • Control over a wide range of EV system components including BMS, inverter, charging and accessory systems
  • Customizable 12V relay control outputs for control over a wide range of relay-switched automotive systems
  • Monitoring and control functionality for application-specific cooling and heating systems
  • Communication with a CAN compatible inverter/drive system to provide drive and diagnostic control for throttle, regenerative braking, and cruise control
  • Provides control of EV system accessories, such as a DC/DC converter, and inverter for accessories like PMSM-Driven-EHPS systems

Safety and Diagnostics

  • ASIL-C safety rated microcontroller, offering monitoring and protection against software and hardware system faults
  • Fault detection of the entire EV system including other systems, sensors, and actuators. User feedback is provided for vehicle maintenance information
  • System monitoring and data collection, including temperature, 12V battery health, and EV system lifecycle information

System Power and Update

  • Low power sleep mode during system shutdown to preserve 12V battery charge and health
  • Multiple modes of system update, including wired updates over CAN bus or wireless updates over 4G-LTE

Inverter

Inverter and DC-DC Converter Functionality Overview


The system offers two configurable high voltage inverters and a DC-DC converter to energize the 12V system of the vehicle in a compact package


Features

  • Built using proven technologies and components
  • Field Oriented Control (FOC) to drive Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motors (PMSM)
  • Control of torque and speed over CAN
  • Live regenerative brake control over CAN for dynamic brake response under different road and load conditions. Hill Assist regenerative feature for maximizing the mechanical brake life and system efficiency.
  • All inverter system parts are automotive rated (AEC-Q100/AEC-Q200 component ratings)

Configurations and Ratings

  • Dual power stage provides the ability to meet the needs of various power system requirements through different system configurations
  1. Independent dual power stage operation providing drive to the main drive system and accessories independently (200A 700V drive and 100A and 700V Accessory)
  2. Parallel configuration for higher power applications (300A, 700V)
  3. Stackable/modular multi-inverter system design allows for dual/multi motor drive configurations
  • Configurable DC-DC converter output for 11-15V (100A typical max). Default state maintains 14V system.
  • Zero power draw in inactive/off mode. 1.2A at 12V draw in active mode.
  • Accurate AC current sensing capabilities (-600A to +600A)

    AC Onboard Charger

    Tailored to our Cold Canadian Climate our onboard charger options act as a traditional block heater to provide auxiliary power for keeping the battery pack warm and fully charged to be ready to operate under any conditions. The seamless integration of third party watercooled OBC with Teal’s VCM makes it simple; Keep me plugged in all year around and I will be ready to serve with no downtime or delays! Popular options are single phase 6.6kW, 13.2kW, and three phase 20kW. Type1 and Type 2 plugs are available options.
