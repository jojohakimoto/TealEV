(1) Electric heat to warmup the cabin increases the energy consumption of a BEV by 20 to 30% in cold climates. In larger metros this can be up to 40% due to heavy traffics. Also current BEVs don’t have winter proof thermal management for battery pack resulting in poor regenerative brake and significant drop in efficiency in cold climates.

(2) With Teal’s range extender onboard, the generator will provide ~35% electric efficiency and ~55% heat efficiency to keep the cabin and battery pack warm under any condition.

(3) Less battery means less recycling related emissions.
